Step 1. After running the app click Dont have an account? Register to go to the account creation page
Step 2. Enter credentials which will be relevant your account and click register
Step 3. you should be taken page to the home login page, if not click already have an account? login
step 4. Book your meeting room by finding the one you want from the ones listed and click Book Now
Step 5. Fill out the date, time, number of people and tick which equipment you need and click confirm booking, a calendar view is also there to help find when a room is free
step 6. you will be taken back to the meeting room page, click My Bookings at the bottom of your screen and you will be shown any rooms you have booked with all the details
Step 7. If you go to the Profile page at the bottom of the screen you will be shown your name, email address and a logout button, if you log out and back in your bookings will still be shown

This will work offline too
